Data Deletion Instructions

If you would like to request the deletion of your data, please follow the steps below:

  1. Submit a request by contacting us at contact@example.com.
  2. Provide your email address and any relevant information related to your account.
  3. Once we process your request, we will delete your data and confirm the deletion within 7 days.
